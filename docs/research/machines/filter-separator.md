# Filter / Separator

## 1. Overview
The filter/separator is a critical piece of equipment in fluid processing pipelines. It removes impurities, particulates, and unwanted media from the process fluid. This ensures product purity and protects downstream equipment from damage.

## 2. Supply Chain Role
- **Category**: Filtration & Separation
- **Function**: To filter out solid contaminants and separate mixed phases (e.g., liquid/gas or oil/water) from the heated flow.
- **Input**: Heated Flow
- **Output**: Filtered Product

## 4. Key Components
1. **Vessel Body**: The main cylindrical housing where the filtration/separation takes place.
2. **Bottom Head**: Often conical to collect and drain impurities or separated fluids.
3. **Top Cover**: Provides access for filter element replacement and maintenance.
4. **Ports**: Inlet and outlet connections for the process fluid to enter and exit.
