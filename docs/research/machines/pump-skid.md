# Pump Skid Research (Flowserve)

## 1. Introduction
A pump skid is a pre-assembled system consisting of one or more pumps, motors, and auxiliary components mounted on a common baseplate. This modular approach allows for easier transportation, installation, and integration into larger fluid processing pipelines.

## 2. Supply Chain Role
* **Company**: Flowserve Corporation (NYSE: FLS)
* **Function**: Provides the essential driving force for fluid movement. It pressurizes the raw material or fluid to push it through the downstream equipment (heat exchangers, filters, and piping).
* **Market Position**: Flowserve is a global leader in providing industrial pumps, valves, and seals, highly utilized in chemical, oil & gas, and water treatment industries.

## 3. Key Components
1. **Baseplate**: A structural frame (often a box-like steel bed) that supports all mounted equipment and ensures proper alignment.
2. **Motor**: The prime mover, usually an electric motor, which provides rotational energy.
3. **Pump Casing / Volute**: The main fluid chamber where impeller rotation converts kinetic energy into fluid pressure.
4. **Flanges**: Connection points for the inlet (suction) and outlet (discharge) piping.

## 4. Token Processing Semantics
* **Input**: `[Raw Material]`
* **Process**: Pressurization (represented by scale pulse or acceleration)
* **Output**: `[Pressurized Flow]`
